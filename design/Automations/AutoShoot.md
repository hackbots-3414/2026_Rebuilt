# Auto Shoot

## Assumptions
* Can check for good launch angles and rotation
* Can know what target is being shot for
* Can know current pose

## Subsystems
* Shooter shoots the fuel

## Steps
* If shooting hub, check robot pose is in our alliance zone. If shooting alliance zone, check which corner is closer and choose to shoot towards that side.
* Check for good rotation and launch angle for given target
* Get shooter up to speed
* Shoot
